Objective

Exploit the reflected input

This demo simulates a search page that writes your query back into the DOM without sanitizing it. Your goal is to inject JavaScript that runs in the victim's browser context and then reveal the flag.

  • Find a payload that executes when the page renders your input.
  • Use the execution to call revealFlag().
  • Once successful, the hidden flag will appear below.
